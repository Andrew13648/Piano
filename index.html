Multisampled Piano

This is a demonstration of a multisampled piano build with Tone.js. You can hear it by either plugging in a midi keyboard, or hit 'PLAY' to hear a midi file.


It uses the Salamander Grand Piano Samples which includes high quality stereo samples of every third key at 5 velocity levels, release harmonics sampled in thirds, damper sounds (on the note release), and sustain pedal sounds. About 200 hundred samples have been combined into a single mp3 which is compressed into 10.9MB.
